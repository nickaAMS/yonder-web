Why Yonder

Everything you wanted from the story box

Starring your child
Every story is written with your child at the centre — their actual name, their companions, their interests woven into the plot.
∞
Never the same twice
New story every night. Adventure, mystery, bedtime quest, dragons, dinosaurs — new every time, forever. No card packs. No repeats.
🌙
Bedtime, not screentime
Stories are read aloud. Phone face-down, lights off — no screen to stare at. Just their voice in the dark, carrying them off to sleep.

Their whole world
Build their story world once: companions, favourites, a gentle villain if they like. Yonder weaves it all in, story after story.

Record a short sample and Yonder narrates every story in your voice. So even when you're not in the room, it still sounds like you.

Any language
Stories in English, Dutch, French, German, Spanish, and more. Perfect for bilingual families or raising little polyglots.
